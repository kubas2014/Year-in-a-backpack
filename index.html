It all began a year ago as an idea to go around the world in a van. So we started considering our options, were looking to buy one, went for some test rides but found out it won’t be as easy as we imagined. Travelling in a van is expensive, logistically complicated and needs experience with mechanics. Eventually, we switched to a more realistic plan to pack our few belongings in a backpack and then see what happens next. We hope that this choice gives us more freedom to travel, the ability to take spontaneous decisions and live on low budget.

So far the plan is to find cheap plane tickets to South America - Chile or Argentina would be the best. Once we’re there, we will get motorbikes, which will be our mean of transport. Heading up north, our aim is to explore the splendid nature, spend time with locals and get a grasp of what it’s like to live there. None of us has ever been to South America and we strive to discover what this region has to offer. Our time frame for the American continent is about 6 months time, after which we plan to fly to Asia from either Seattle or Vancouver. Another 6+ months in Asia, we want explore as many countries and cultures as we can but this is way ahead.

Why Year in a Backpack?

Travelling makes me really happy and exploring the world has always been my dream. That's why I want to do this!

We want to 'take' you on our journey with us. We believe that we will see a lot of interesting places, meet inspiring people and do incredible things.

This adventure will be shared with you online on our social media and this website. We promise to take loads of pictures, write up the experiences we are going through and upload occasional vlogs.

It sounds easy to spend a year in a backpack but we know it will be much harder to carry this idea out.

For this reason, your help would be greatly appreciated! We are happy for any form of support such as your travel tips, having a place to sleep, showing us around or anything else you can think of!
