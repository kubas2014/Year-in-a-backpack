Our story

Hello! We are two friends from Prague, Kuba and Simon, who decided to travel for one year.

Jakub is a fresh Bristol graduate, pretty happy when with his backpack and discovering something somewhere.

Simon has recently opened up a bar in Prague with his friends - he's always happy to grab beer and meet with new people.

We saved up money from work to finance this trip. We travel on a low budget, with everything we need in our backpacks. We never know where we'd end up sleeping and our plans change often. Our travel is spontaneous, fun and punk.

We share our experiences on this blog so keep an eye on stories, pictures, videos and much more from our journey. Interested in knowing more about us and the trip?
